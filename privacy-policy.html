Categories of data collected

The type of data we collect depends on how you use the Services. Typically, we collect the following data:

  • Identifiers/contact information: name, username, gamertag, postal and email address, phone number, unique identifiers, mobile device ID, platform ID, game service ID, advertising ID (IDFA, Android ID), and IP address.
  • Protected characteristics: age and gender
  • Commercial information: purchase and usage history, and preferences, including gaming information.
  • Billing information: payment information (credit/debit card information) and shipping address.
  • Web activity/Electronic activity: web browsing/applications and gaming information associated with the Services; information about your online interactions with the Services or our advertising; as well as information about the games and platforms you use and other information related to installed applications.
  • Device and usage data: device type, software and hardware information, language settings, browser type and version, operating system, and information about how users use and interact with the Services (such as content viewed, pages viewed, clicks, scrolls).
  • Profile-based inferences: the inferences drawn from your information and online activities help us create a personalized profile that allows us to identify products and services that may be of interest to you.
  • Audio-visual information: photographs, images and account avatars, audio information through features and chat functions, and game recordings and video sequences (for example, while participating in game tests).
  • Confidential information: precise location information (if you allow the Services to collect your location), credentials (username and password), and the content of messages through chat and chat functionality.

How we use the information and the legal bases

We use the information collected by the World of Digital Technology group of companies in various ways for the following purposes:

  • Business objectives: Provide services and support; fulfillment of orders and requests; improve the Services and our business; development of new products and services; improve your experience when using the Services; protect the safety of our users, employees, facilities, and Services; as well as the development of internal marketing and demographic research.
  • Commercial purposes: Promote and advertise our products and services, the products and services of our business partners; send you promotional materials; as well as organize promotions or events.

We also use your information in accordance with the law.

We use the information we collect for business purposes, which include:

  • Provision of services;
  • fulfill product orders and other requests, answer your questions and communicate with you;
  • improve and personalize your experience when using the Services, including recognizing you, maintaining your preferences and settings, allowing communications between you and other users, and connecting to your third-party platforms, games, and social networks;
  • provide technical and other support;
  • test the use and performance of the Services, develop new products or services, and perform analysis to improve our software, content, marketing, support, and Services;
  • conduct internal demographic and marketing research;
  • solve problems with the Services or implement other business requirements;
  • protect the security or integrity of the Services, users, and our business, for example, protecting and preventing violations of our terms and policies, including fighting fraud, piracy, deception, counterfeiting, unauthorized transactions, claims, and other liabilities, and risk management;
  • interact on your behalf with third-party platforms, games, and social networks that you connect to our Services;
  • participate in other processing about which we inform you when you provide your information or when you give your consent to such processing; in accordance with the law.

We use the information we collect for business purposes, which include:

  • provide personalized content, specific offers, and advertising about the Services to you and other users via email and text messages or on other websites, mobile applications, or social networks on behalf of World of Digital Technology or our business partners;
  • contact you to provide information and promotional materials;
  • administer bonus programs, surveys, sweepstakes, contests, or other promotions or events sponsored or operated by us or our business partners;
  • Analyse our users in combination with other data to evaluate our user base and direct marketing to other similar groups.

—Legal basis for the processing of personal data

If your location requires us to provide a legal basis, we process your information based on:

  • our celebration and execution of a contract with you (such as our terms of service and when we need to take certain actions at your request, such as completing transactions with you or allowing you to play our online games);
  • compliance with legal obligations;
  • for our legitimate interests or the legitimate interests of our partners, for example, to improve our products and services; perform analysis to better understand our users; carry out direct marketing (where we do not require your consent); otherwise support our business, operations and services; and to enforce our terms or policies and prevent violations;
  • protect your vital interests or the interests of another person (for example, another player);
  • with your consent, for example, when you register to receive direct marketing communications or link your World of Digital Technology account to a service provided by a third party.

Sources of information we collect

The way you receive information depends on how you use the Services. For example, we collect information:

  • that you provide us directly, such as when you create an account, register for marketing purposes or communicate with us;
  • about the games you play (automatically);
  • from third-party platforms, such as when you play our games or access our Services using third-party accounts;
  • automatically, even through the use of cookies and similar tracking technologies;
  • from third parties, such as advertising networks, business partners, third-party information providers and other actors.

—Information you provide

You provide us with information when you use the Services, for example, when:

  • create an account to use or utilize the Services;
  • subscribe to newsletters and communications;
  • leave comments on our electronic messaging system, forums, chats, channels, websites or when you use other Services;
  • purchase or activate one of our products or services, including real and virtual products, virtual currency or subsequent additions;
  • download, install or access demo versions of products, programs or other software;
  • contact us for support or other purposes;
  • use our email or communication features;
  • participate in events, competitions, promotions, surveys or game tests;
  • participate in other events under the terms of which you provide us with your information.

Depending on the Services or your activities, we collect information such as your name, email address, phone number, photo, postal address/postal code, payment or purchase information, age, gender, password, platform ID, reproducible software products, survey data, geolocation and the systems or platforms on which you play. We combine this information across all the devices you use.

When you use the Services, we automatically receive information about your game. The game information includes your platform ID or game service ID, game achievements, game results and statistics, IP address, MAC address or other device identifiers, other platform/device information and other information and statistics related to your use of the Services. We collect game information even if you have not registered to use the Services. We may combine game information with other information we have about you. We can also use internal and third-party anti-fraud technologies to detect and prevent fraud in our Services.

We collect information when you use third-party platforms to connect and use our Services, such as a game platform account, a game network service account or a social network service account. When you access our Services in this way, third-party platforms provide us with information about you. The third-party platform may allow you to control the information that is provided to us. We are not responsible for the terms of use and policies governing the use of third-party platforms, nor for any disclosure or other actions on third-party platforms.

We may use cookies, web beacons, pixel tags, Flash cookies, site logs, software development kits and similar tracking technologies to improve and maintain the functionality of our Services, enhance security and prevent fraud, and provide you with targeted advertising. Upon receiving information from third parties, we update the information we collect about you. We may obtain information from publicly available databases, platform manufacturers, analytics providers, game developers and other business partners, as well as from the World of Digital Technology family of companies involved in providing you with the Services, which includes demographic information and information about your interests. We may collect publicly available information, even from social networks, to protect our users, employees, facilities and Services, and to assist with investigations. We also receive information from other people who use our Services. Specifically, we may receive information about you if you are mentioned in comments about the Services or if a person sends us a message directly.

As indicated below, we share your information, if you agreed previously:

  • to third parties (for example, to prepare joint proposals, for legal and security purposes, in connection with the sale or transfer of business or assets, and for other purposes with your permission);
  • for other purposes, such as when you participate in public communities or events or when you post information on leaderboards.

World of Digital Technology transfers unique identifiers, IP addresses, web/email activity data and inferences based on profiles to third-party advertising service providers to provide you and others like you with personalized advertising. We do not sell or share the personal information of any person who has not given their approval for it.

Rights regarding your data

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights in relation to your personal data, including the right to:

  • submit a request to access your personal data or obtain a copy of such data;
  • submit a request to delete your personal data;
  • submit a request to clarify or change your personal data;

  • withdraw your consent if consent for processing has been obtained from you;
  • prohibit the sale or transfer of your personal information (including targeted advertising);
  • accept or refuse to receive financial incentives;
  • limit the use and disclosure of your confidential personal information.

You can make a request about your rights over your personal data here. We will respond to data requests within a month. We may take steps to verify your identity before responding to a request. In some cases, these rights (if they apply to you) may be limited, for example, when we must comply with legal requirements or we have a compelling interest in processing your personal data, or when personal data is protected by trade secrets. If you have unresolved issues, where applicable, you may also have the right to file a complaint with a data protection authority. A list of data protection authorities is available here. If you wish to make a data request or appeal a decision regarding a data request, please contact World of Digital Technology using the contact information provided in the Contact Us section.

To opt out of the sale or transfer of personal information, click on the “Do not sell or share my personal information” button at the bottom of any page on our site (and available here) and follow the instructions on that page. To opt out of receiving personalized online advertising in general, see Manage your account, messages and data for information about your opt-out options. We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of these rights.

You can authorize any person to make a request on your behalf regarding your privacy rights (authorized agent). We reserve the right to ask you directly for confirmation that the agent is authorized to make such a request or to request additional information to verify the agent’s identity.

Data storage

We will retain your information as long as we provide you with the Services and as necessary to comply with our legal obligations and/or exercise, defend or establish our rights.
We retain your information while your account is active, while we provide you with the Services, and for as long as necessary to administer our Services. If your account is inactive, we will keep it for the period of time during which we reasonably expect you to use our Services again. After this period, we will delete personal data, including your email address and login credentials, and you will no longer be able to access your account. We may still retain some of your personal data in our files for a reasonable period of time to: resolve disputes, enforce our policies, terms, and conditions; administration of our Services, compliance with technical and legal requirements and/or other restrictions related to the security, integrity, and operation of our Services, after which we will take steps to delete or archive your personal data.
